<div align="justify">
Dr. Prabu M is an Indian academician who is serving as an <b>Assistant Professor [Sr.Gr]</b> in the <b>Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Amrita School of Computing, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Chennai.</b> He has 14 years of experience in teaching and research. <br><br>

Dr. Prabu M is an alumnus of <b>VIT University, Vellore</b>, Tamil Nadu, India, where he completed his Doctoral – Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering with support from <b>ISRO-SAC, Ahmedabad </b>. He has authored research papers that have been published in renowned international journals and conferences, and are indexed in both SCOPUS and SCI. His research primarily focuses on Computer Vision and Distributed Computing. He has served as a resource person and speaker at several conferences and workshops. <br><br>

He is a Life Member of ISDS Society – International Society for Development and Sustainability, IAENG International Association of Engineers, ISTE-Life Member Indian Society for Technical Education, Internet Society, IFERP-Institute for Engineering Research, and Publication. He holds an online certifications from Coursera, NPTEL and he has completed all the modules in the National Institute of Technical Teachers Training (NITTT) offered by AICTE.
</div>
