<p align="justify">
Dr. Prabu M is an Assistant Professor in the School of Computing Science and Engineering at VIT Bhopal University. He is an alumnus of VIT University, Vellore, where he obtained a Ph.D. in Distributed Satellite Data Processing with support from ISRO-SAC, Ahmedabad. Dr. Prabu M has an academic and research experience of 12 years and has worked at various levels. During his career, he has also worked on ISRO-SAC and DST-funded projects. Dr. Prabu M has filed patents and published papers, book chapter in various peer-reviewed International Journals, and Conferences indexing with Scopus, SCI, and WoS. He is also one of the book editors entitled Outcomes of Best Practices in Classroom Research and also he is a reviewer in several reputed International Scopus and SCI-indexed journals. Dr. Prabu M has been an expert speaker in various Faculty Development Programmes and contributed to several International Conferences, Workshops. He is an active member of various international bodies like Life Member of ISDS -International Society for Development and Sustainability, IAENG- International Association of Engineers, ISTE-Life Member of Indian Society for Technical Education, Internet Society, IFERP-Institute for Engineering Research, and Publication. His research interests include Computer Vision, Distributed Computing, Machine Learning, Remote Sensing, and GIS. </p>
